	For any prime number _p_ and any positive integer _n_, let νp(n)![{\displaystyle \nu _{p}(n)}](https://wikimedia.org/api/rest_v1/media/math/render/svg/a740c63898dac75666e43736eb3ae87eb8c62b16) be the exponent of the largest power of _p_ that divides _n_ (that is, the [_p_-adic valuation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/P-adic_valuation "P-adic valuation") of _n_). Then

$$
{\displaystyle \nu _{p}(n!)=\sum _{i=1}^{\infty }\left\lfloor {\frac {n}{p^{i}}}\right\rfloor }
$$
