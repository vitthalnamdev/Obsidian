When a single CPU run multiple processes at a same time , by switching between process , this technique is generally called time sharing of the CPU.
This is achieved by <b>Virtualization</b> 


<h1>Context Switching</h1>
This is another mechanism (i.e. a low level method) , which gives OS the ability to stop running a process and start running some other process , this time sharing mechanism is employed by every modern OS's

High - level Intelligence involves , some policies based on what the OS make decision , of which process to run first , and for what period of time.
